The Siberian tiger, also called Amur tiger, is a species of tiger that lives mainly the Sikhote Alin mountain region in southwest Primorye Province in Russia. They are mammals that weigh 660lbs and they are about 10.75ft tall. They are currently an endangered species and their population is being stabilized because of conservative efforts to preserve them.
